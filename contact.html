Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How does InsightsHive work?

Our mobile app captures in-store imagery, AI processes the images to extract KPIs, and our web platform delivers real-time insights and analytics.

❓ What KPIs can you extract?

We extract Out-of-Stock rates, Share of Shelf metrics, Planogram Compliance, promotional display effectiveness, and competitive product presence.

❓ How accurate is your AI?

Our AI achieves 95%+ accuracy in product recognition and KPI extraction, significantly outperforming manual methods.

❓ Can you integrate with our existing systems?

Yes, our platform offers API integrations and can connect with most retail management and ERP systems.

❓ What's the implementation timeline?

Typical implementation takes 2-4 weeks, including training, customization, and integration with your existing workflows.

❓ Do you offer pilot programs?

Yes, we offer pilot programs for qualified retailers to test our platform in a limited number of locations before full deployment.
